# 🌟 E-MOODY – Mood Improvement & Tracking App  

**E-MOODY** is an innovative mood-tracking application designed to help individuals monitor and improve their emotional well-being. Recognizing and managing emotions is crucial for mental health, as suppressing emotions can lead to stress, anxiety, and even depression. With **E-MOODY**, users can actively track their moods daily, visualize their emotional patterns, and take steps toward mental wellness.  

By **analyzing daily surveys and generating reports**, this application empowers users to take responsibility for their emotions, which can ultimately **enhance productivity** in both personal and professional settings. The app also ensures **smooth performance and reliability**, minimizing software malfunctions and glitches through continuous monitoring.  

---

## 🚀 Key Features  

✅ **Daily & Monthly Mood Surveys** – Track emotions consistently.  
✅ **Achievements System** – Encourages user engagement and goal-setting.  
✅ **Real-time Calendar** – Visualize mood trends over time.  
✅ **Mood Tracking & Scaling** – Understand emotional fluctuations.  
✅ **Daily User Status on Graph** – Get insightful analytics of mood progress.  
✅ **Hint Button** – Provides helpful tips for emotional management.  
✅ **Data Management** – Delete previous data or continue unfinished entries.
